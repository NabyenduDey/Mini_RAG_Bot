# Acme Corp — HR & conduct (sample)

## Remote work

Employees may work remotely up to three days per week after completing the 90-day onboarding period. Manager approval is required for a fixed schedule. Core collaboration hours are 10:00–15:00 local time.

## Time off

Request paid time off at least 10 business days in advance via the HR portal. Emergency absences should be reported to your manager and hr-oncall@example.com the same day.

## Expenses

Submit receipts within 14 days. Client meals require a business purpose note. Personal subscriptions are not reimbursable.
