# Café Acme — simple recipes (sample)

## Overnight oats

Combine ½ cup rolled oats, ½ cup milk or oat milk, 1 tbsp chia seeds, and sweetener to taste. Refrigerate overnight. Top with berries before serving.

## One-pan lentil soup

Sauté onion and carrot, add 1 cup red lentils, 4 cups vegetable broth, bay leaf, and cumin. Simmer 25 minutes until lentils break down. Finish with lemon juice and parsley.

## Banana smoothie

Blend one ripe banana, 1 cup yogurt, ½ cup ice, and a pinch of cinnamon. Optional: add peanut butter for protein.
